Job Introduction
In general the function of the Engineering Support is to assist the Tunnel Engineering team in the engineering management of the various tunnel works. In particular this will consist of:

Assisting with the preparation of tunnel specific documentation to enable the works to progress without delay
Collate data to support the implementation of the PTE process including providing tunnel drive specific input into the SRG meetings
Assist with Engineering administrative tasks to support the various engineering functions
Provide stand-in-cover for the Tunnel Engineers.
Conduct tunnel visits for data collection and review.


The role will report to the Tunnel Engineering Manager.

Main Responsibilities
Contribute to the Shift Review Group (SRG) and Permit to Excavate (PTE) process, including the technical review of ground movements from the tunnelling works and reconciling the ground movements against TBM parameters.
Contributing to the QA function for the tunnel drives and ensuring that the required assurance evidence is obtained and stored correctly.
Manage progressive assurance for tunnel segments by creating and reviewing documentation packages
Being a focal point between the various 3rd parties / utilities teams and the tunnel delivery team, ensuring that 3rd party approvals are actioned within the SRG as necessary.
Ensuring that engineering aspects of tunnel management are captured in the programme
Engineering review of technical submissions
Assist to review instrumentation and monitoring data for surface movements due to tunnelling.
Review of Ground Movement Assessments to understand predicted movements of third party assets.
The Ideal Candidate
Engineering degree - working towards Chartership
Tunnelling experience preferable but not essential
